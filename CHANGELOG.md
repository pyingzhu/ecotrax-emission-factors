## [2026-02] — 2026-02-22

### Added
- Initial public release
- `food_beverage` — 22 items from Agribalyse 3.1 (ADEME)
- `transport` — 13 passenger modes from Mobitool.ch 2023 / DEFRA 2023
- `waste` — 10 treatment methods from WRAP UK 2023
- `utilities` — 12 factors covering electricity (5 grids), natural gas, LPG, diesel generators, water supply/treatment, district heating
- `_scope` metadata per category (Scope 1 / 2 / 3 + LCA boundary)
